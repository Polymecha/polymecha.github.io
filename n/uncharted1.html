Thoughts on Uncharted 1

Uncharted: Drake's Fortune is a great game, the characters and their lines are kind of cheesy, but that's part of why it's good. The interactions between characters are a bit unrealistic, but that adds to the experience of the game.

It's generally a really fun game though, with a great story and great characters, even with some of the cheesy lines. You should definitely try it if you haven't already, but even if you have, it's enjoyable to replay it. It's definitely a game worth your money.

Although I kind of wished it would be available on PC, it wasn't any issue using an emulator to play it.
