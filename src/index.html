Tilt

Tilt is an open-world game featuring an infinite world where players can explore diverse structures such as tombs, colosseums, portals, and occasional boss fights. The game offers a wide range of exploration opportunities and hidden treasures for players to discover. The structures found in the game contribute to the immersive experience and provide a glimpse into the game's lore and overarching narrative.

Nopism HQ

Nopism HQ, Dubbed "The Modular Atmosphere Project" aimed to investigate the potential impact of a modular atmospheric environment on human cognitive processes. The game utilized controlled experimental conditions to simulate different atmospheric modules and examined their effects on the participants' brains. This proof-of-concept experiment serves as a starting point for further research and exploration into the intricate relationship between atmospheric conditions and cognitive functioning.
