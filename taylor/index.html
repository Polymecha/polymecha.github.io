Hello! My name is Polymecha, and I'm excited to introduce you to Taylor, an AI-powered Discord chatbot. Taylor is a project I've been working on to bring a unique and interactive experience to Discord.

About Taylor

Taylor is an advanced AI chatbot designed to engage with users in Discord through direct messages and group chats. Taylor can:

  • Respond to user messages intelligently.
  • Analyze and describe images sent by users.
  • Initiate conversations with top users periodically.
  • Interact in group chats to keep the conversation flowing.
  • Join voice channels and play random audio clips.

How It Works

Using a combination of AI models and custom scripts, Taylor can understand and respond to user inputs in a natural and engaging way. The bot maintains a short-term memory for ongoing conversations and a leaderboard to keep track of user interactions. You can contact me if you want to learn more.

Why Taylor?

The goal of this project is to explore the capabilities of AI in social interactions and to create a fun and engaging experience for Discord users. The initial purpose was to see how real you could make a Discord user look.
