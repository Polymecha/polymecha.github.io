How It Works

Taylor uses a mix of AI models and custom scripts to understand and respond to what you say naturally. She keeps a short-term memory for ongoing chats and even has a leaderboard to track interactions. If you want to know more, feel free to ask!

Why Taylor?

I started this project to see how well AI can mimic social interactions and to create a fun experience for Discord users. It's kind of an experiment to see how real a Discord user can feel.
